About the author

I am a highschool student from Philippine Science Highschool Southern Mindanao Campus. My name is Josh Ethan C. Mercado and I am 15 years old.

I chose this topic of advocacy because it hurts my heart that there are people who have less than I do and are grateful for even the tiniest blessing. Compared to me, who doesn't realize what something is worse until I don't have it. I believe that they should have a chance to have an education, to not worry about food every night, and to have a future where they themselves can provide for their own families.

Some of the links used in this site belong to a different organization where you can actually help people by donating or partnering with them to setup charity events and whatnot.
